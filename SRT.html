The Sponsored Research Team (SRT) is excited to provide a number of benefits to Principal Investigators (PIs) in the College of Engineering and Information Technology (COEIT). Our primary goal is to provide streamlined pre- and post- award support that is grounded in excellent customer service. Pre-award support includes providing proposal development and routing services, specifically preparation of budgets, subawards, and adherence to proposal and regulatory requirements. Post-award support includes account reconciliations, effort reporting, and contract management for any sponsored research projects including startup, revolving, foundation, and grants. Essentially, we steward your proposal through submission, account set-up, award administration, and award close-out.

Please visit our SSC Tasks or our FAQs for additional information. If you're unable to find an answer to your question, please submit your question or any feedback you may have through the General Inquires RT ticketing system. Select your respective Division, Department, then select "General Inquires" from the Request Type drop down menu.

We look forward to serving you!
